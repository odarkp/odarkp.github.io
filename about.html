"The Band"

Part band, part art project, the group mixes music, glitchy visuals, and internet-inspired aesthetics into one weird little universe. Known for their atmospheric sound and visually immersive aesthetic, the band blends alternative pop, electronic production, and experimental visuals to create a unique multimedia experience. Their work explores themes of identity, nostalgia, and digital culture, aiming to connect music, design, and storytelling into one cohesive artistic world.
